## **HIPAA Privacy Practices**

Houston Cardiology Consultants is fully committed to protecting your health information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). This notice outlines how your medical information may be used and disclosed, and how you can access this information.

---

### **How We May Use Your Health Information**

We may use or disclose your protected health information (PHI) for the following purposes:

- **Treatment** – Sharing necessary information with other healthcare providers involved in your care (e.g., specialists, labs, imaging centers).
- **Payment** – Submitting claims to your insurance company, verifying benefits, and billing you or a third-party payer.
- **Healthcare Operations** – For internal management such as quality assessment, training, and improving services.

---

### **Other Permitted Disclosures**

- **When Required by Law** – Including public health reporting, abuse reporting, or law enforcement.
- **To Prevent Serious Threats** – We may disclose your PHI to prevent serious harm to you or others.
- **Business Associates** – With contracted partners who perform services for us (e.g., billing or IT providers), under strict confidentiality agreements.
- **De-identified Data Use** – In some cases, information stripped of identifying data may be used for research or operational improvements.

---

### **Your HIPAA Rights**

You have the right to:

- **Inspect and Receive Copies of Your Health Record** – Usually within 30 days of your request.
- **Request Corrections** – If you believe your records are incorrect or incomplete.
- **Receive an Accounting of Disclosures** – Detailing non-routine uses of your PHI.
- **Request Confidential Communications** – Specify a preferred contact method (e.g., phone, email).
- **Request Restrictions** – On the use or disclosure of certain information.
- **File a Complaint** – Without fear of retaliation, to us or directly to the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS).

---

### **Our Responsibilities**

We are required by law to:

- Maintain the privacy and security of your PHI.
- Provide you with this notice and follow the terms described within.
- Notify you if a breach occurs that may compromise the privacy or security of your information.
